EC:Rainy day activities

Posted on 2017-06-26 | In Life

Summary

In this week, our topic is talking about the rainy day activities. And Carl’s statement before topic as follows:

Hey,Gang,

My God, but it’s been raining a lot lately. That makes it a good time for rainy day activities. What are your favorite rainy day activities? Going to a museum? Staying home with a good book? Do you like to go to the movies? What outdoor activities are good when it’s raining? Most of them are less crowded in inclement weather. Inclement? What’s that mean?

We’ll be talking about rainy days, how to talk about rainy days, and what we like to do on them.

See you Thursday!

Carl

I was late in this week because my team had a meeting in the Thursday’s afternoon, so I didn’t have a chance to talk with Carl face to face. I thought I should go to EC earlier than this time so that I can get the chance to talk with Carl.

Group Talks

This week group talks was talking about what activities would we like in raniny day. There was a old staff in our group. She had been employed by our campany for nine years. She was a very interesting and passional member in our group. You know, I didn’t know why these guys had many activities to do in rainy days, because I thought it is very comfortable to stay at home when rainy days. It’s so uncomfortable when you go out in rainy days because you will get wet. But this staff, she was a little different from us. She is very good at some sports. She said she will do some housework or cook in home, but she preferred do some exercise like tennis or swiming and other indoor sports when the rainy days. She was so interesting for us. And I thought she is a model for our learning.

What’s more, a girl in our group also like to stay at home and do some things like me. Just like play some mobile games, you know, HONOR OF THE KINGS (may be called STRIKE OF KINGS) is so popular in these days.

And another girl, she like do some cooking and played with her cat named Lucky. She had show her cat’s photo and we all thought it is very cute. She said she had fed her cat about six years it is just like 60 years old in humans. I thought this girl is so lucky for it. And I want to feed a cat too.

And there a boy, he like play basketball in the weekend. And I told to him I want to join them, he is so welcome me and let me scan their wechat QR code to join them.

By the way, I saw a new girl in this weeks’ EC. And she looked like Emily, the role in FRIENDS. Maybe I will have a chance to tell her my thought in next time.
