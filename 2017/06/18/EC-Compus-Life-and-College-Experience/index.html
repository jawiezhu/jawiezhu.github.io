EC:Compus Life and College Experience

Posted on 2017-06-18 | In Life

Summary

Today, we have another English teacher Sasha for our English corner.

Firstly, he asked our names and let us introduced ourselves to him. And then talk about some campus life in his student time. He is interesting, he grew up in SheKou, although he is American. A funny thing is he had the Chinese character SheKou as a tattoo in his right thigh. He is from Washington DC, so he is a Wizards’ fan. And funny thing is he don’t like Warriors, don’t like Stephen Curry. He and I are all like Kobe Bryant. We thought Kobe is so handsome no matter shoot or free throw. And we admire Kobe’s spirit that he always want to win the game.

What’s more, I talk about the John Wall with him. He is very like John Wall, although John Wall and Wizards are not hot in China. I tell John Wall and Bradley Beal are called Two Guns in Backcourt in our Chinese basketball forum Hoop just like Reddit.

Group Talks

Today’s group talks is about talk something most regret or most embarrassing thing in our campus.

There are 4 people in our group, 2 girls,1 boy and I.

The topic start from me. Firstly, I don’t know what should I talk, and few minutes later. I started to talk the most embarrassing thing in my campus life.When I was a freshmen in college, I met a girl and had a crush on her. She was very beautiful in my eyes and she had the short hair just like Kwai Lun Mei. But very unlucky that I knew a bad news for me. My friend told me she was a lesbian. And I was very shocked in that time, because I never ever met this situation in real life. So it was my most embarrassing thing in my campus life.

And the second talker is another boy. He have graduated a very long time ago. And he told us his campus life is so normal and just eating, sleeping, playing games.

And the third talker is a girl. Maybe she was a little shy in our group talks. She said she is a very very normal student in campus. And she was so well-behaved and just had classes, did some part-time jobs. And that’s all.

Last talker is a girl too. This girl is so interesting and outgoing. She told us she liked a boy when she was in undergraduate. That boy was so excellent and always got No.1 in his major. And one day she can’t hide her love and she told that boy I like you. But very unfortunately, that boy rejected her love. She was so sad in that time. But now, she actually thanks to that boy. She said she become better and try her best in every aspect because of him. Although that boy is working in Silicon Valley, they still keep touch in some time.

So, in the end of group talks, we all agree that our campus life is very precious in our life no matter is happy or unhappy. And thank to someone gives a very good memory in our life.
